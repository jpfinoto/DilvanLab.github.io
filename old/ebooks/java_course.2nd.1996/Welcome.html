Distributed Programming using Java


This is the introduction to the course: Topics in Electronics I - Distributed Programming using Java. The goal of the WWW pages of this course is to offer students all the material about the course in a format more learner friendly. Here you can find a guide with the contents of the course, all the transparencies shown at lectures and complementary material in the form of papers, auxiliary texts and links to WWW pages with related material.

Keep in mind that these WWW pages are an evolving tool. They may have mistakes and be subject to big changes as the course progresses. I hope you enjoy this new teaching tool and help to make it work.

Why is it written in English?: You may ask why didn't I wrote the course in Portuguese. I can give you two reasons: I had a lot of the material already written in English (and no time or disposition to translate it), and the fact that everybody working with computer nowadays understands written English.
