1. Basic Laboratory Tools

The main goal of this experimental class is to make you familiar with basic laboratory tools and circuit techniques. You should master the following topics by reading the associated material before the class:

1- Continuous current circuits:

Book: Eletrônica Básica - J. Brophy - Chapter 1 - pages 1 to 13.

Book: Eletrônica Vol 1 - Malvino - Chapter 1 - pages 1 to 9.

2- Equipment used in the experiments:

Book: Eletrônica Básica - J. Brophy - Chapter 1 - pages 25 to 31.

Anexo da prática: Multimeter, Function Generator, Power Supply, Reading Resistors, Protoboards.

Complementary reading:

Book: Teoria e Desenvolvimento de Projetos de Circuitos Eletrônicos - Cipelli & Sandrini - pages 15 to 18.
