2. Osciloscope Use

The main goal of this experimental class is to make you familiar with the use of the oscilloscope and with circuit techniques like the Kirchhoff law. You should master the following topics by reading the associated material before the class:

  1. Circuits' Laws:

    Book: Eletrônica Básica - J. Brophy - Chapter 1 - pages 13 to 25.

    Book: Eletrônica Vol 1 - Malvino - Chapter 1 - pages 9 to 21.

  2. Equipment used in the experiments:

    Book: Eletrônica Básica - J. Brophy - Chapter 2 - pages 64 to 68.
