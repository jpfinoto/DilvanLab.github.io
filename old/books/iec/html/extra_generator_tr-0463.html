3. Function Generator TR-0463

Similar ao IT-100K com alguns recursos adicionais. O modo de operação é também similar ao do IT-100K.

3.1. Identificação dos Controles

Figure 4.6. Gerador de Funções TR-0463

FREQ      1- A row of nine push-buttons, for the coarse
             selection 
             of the basic generator frequency. 
          2- Dial of continuous frequency variation. 
FUNCTION  3- Three push-buttons for waveform selection. 
OFFSET    4- Potentiometer for continuous DC offset. 
ON        5- Push-button for switching on and off the DC
             offset. 
AMPL      6- Potentiometer for continuous amplitude
             variation. 
          7- Three push-buttons for amplitude selection in
             steps of 20 dB. 
SYM       8- Potentiometer for varying the time symmetry of 
             the triangular signal and the duty cycle of the
             pulse. 
VAR       9- Control 8 is only effective when this
             push-button is depressed. 
TRIG OUT  10- The sync signal provided by the generator is 
              available at this BNC connector. 
OUT       11- The output signal of the generator is
              available at this BNC connector. 
MODULATOR 12- RCYW of function selector push-buttons. 
    EXT       Depressed when an external signal is used for 
              driving the generator 
    AM        Amplitude modulation 
    FM        Frequency modulation 
    SWP       Sweep mode 
    TRIG      Triggered mode 
    GATE      Gated mode 
    OFF       Switching-off of controlled and modulation
              modes. 
FREQ      13- Potentiometer for continuous variation of the 
              modulation generator frequency. 
          14- Three push-buttons for frequency band
              selection. 
AMPL      15- Potentiometer for adjustment of the modulation 
              generator signal amplitude. 
FUNCTION  16- Row of push-buttons for selecting the signal 
              waveform of the modulation generator. 
SYM       17- Potentiometer for adjustment of the time
              symmetry of the modulation signal. 
EXT IN    18- BNC connector for receiving an external 
              modulation signal/gate pulse, trigger pulse/. 
MOD OUT   19- Output of internal modulation generator. 
POWER     20- Led indicating the switched-on state.
