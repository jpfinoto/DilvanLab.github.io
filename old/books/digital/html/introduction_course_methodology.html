1. Course Methodology

This course is centered in 8 modules. Each module will have a group of students in charge of it and will last for 4 lectures. Each group will give 3 lectures (except the first group that will give only 2) covering the theoretical material assigned for its module and apply a test (in the 4th day) about this theoretical material. Before the test, another group (a review group) will review the work that was presented and present extra teaching material, if they think this material was missing in the lectures. The group can challenger the review group's critics.

The tests will be graded by the group in charge of the module and reviewed by the course monitor.

For each module there will be recommended text that should be read by every student BEFORE each lecture.

All the material (teaching material and reviews) should be presented in HTML (the Word editor can save in this format) or PowerPoint format and handed in using the electronic tools provided.

The groups in charge of each module should upload the material for the lectures before the day of the class. This material should be in the HTML or PowerPoint format.

FINAL GRADE (Theory)

Each student final grade will be the average of:

  1. His group's grade.

  2. The average of his tests grades.
