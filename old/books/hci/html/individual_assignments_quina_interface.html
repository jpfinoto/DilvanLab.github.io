1. Quina Interface

Construct a functional mockup of the interface for a system to generate bets for the Quina game. This system will run in a Palm OS PDA (Personal Digital Assistant), such as Palm or Handspring PDAs, and thus the mock up should use the Palm OS interface widgets.

The scenery could be a bar or a restaurant where clients would have Palm OS PDAs available to them to make Quina games. When the clients ask their bill, the price of their bets would be added to their bill and they would receive the receipts of their bets.

Remember:

  1. Take in consideration the size of a Palm OS PDA when design your interface.

  2. You can make the mockup in any suitable language (Java, HTML, etc). However, you may get a better result using a Palm OS emulator (for Windows or Linux). If do not use a emulator, remember that you should use the Palm OS interface widgets.

  3. You have to worry only about the interface, you do not have to implement other parts of the system, such as the communication system for sending the bets to a computer terminal. The mockup should give a feeling of how the system operation will be, you do not have to implement the actual operations.

  4. Get a Quina ticket from a Loterica to know how it looks like and what options are available. Remember, your users are already familiar with its appearance.
