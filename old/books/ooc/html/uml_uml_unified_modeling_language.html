1. UML - Unified Modeling Language

The UML is standard diagramming language to visualize the results of analysis and design.

  • An emerging standard for modeling object-oriented software.

  • Resulted from the convergence of notations from three leading object-oriented methods:

    • OMT (James Rumbaugh)

    • OOSE (Ivar Jacobson)

    • Booch (Grady Booch)

  • UML Notation is simple and relatively trivial.

  • Much more important: Skill in designing with objects.

The UML is not:

  • A process or methodology.

  • Object-oriented analysis and design.

  • Guideline for design.
